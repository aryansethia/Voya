Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my VOYA experience?+
We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability and proper preparation of your mystery experience. For peak seasons (October-March), booking 4-6 weeks ahead is ideal.
What information do you need from me to plan my mystery adventure?+
We'll need your travel dates, fitness level, dietary restrictions, accommodation preferences, and any activities you absolutely want to avoid. This helps us craft the perfect mystery while keeping some elements secret.
Can I contact you during my mystery adventure if needed?+
Absolutely! You'll receive 24/7 emergency contact numbers and local guide contacts. Our support team is always available to assist with any concerns during your journey.
Do you offer group discounts for mystery experiences?+
Yes! We offer special rates for groups of 4 or more travelers. Contact us directly to discuss group pricing and customized mystery experiences for your party.
